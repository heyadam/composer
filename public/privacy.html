Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Composer ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our visual AI workflow builder service at composer.design (the "Service").

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile information through Google OAuth.
  • User Content: We collect the workflows, prompts, and other content you create using the Service.
  • API Keys: If you provide your own API keys for AI providers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic), these are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you interact with the Service, including features used and actions taken.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Process and store your workflows and content
  • Communicate with you about the Service
  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services including Supabase for authentication and data storage.
  • AI Providers: When you execute workflows, prompts are sent to your configured AI providers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic) according to their respective privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. Workflow data is stored in Supabase with appropriate access controls. API keys stored in your browser are encrypted locally and never transmitted to our servers.

Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information
  • Delete your workflows and content
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Close your account at any time

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and improve the Service. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with third-party AI providers. Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@composer.design.
